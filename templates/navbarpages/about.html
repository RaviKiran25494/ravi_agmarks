FARMER

Agmarks Benefits to Farmers

Crop Seeding

Crop trends in the area for the year to take informed decision. Upon posting requirement, seeds are sent to farmers from verified companies to avoid low quality yield and grade.

Fertilizers

Upon seeding, Agmarks suggest required fertilizers based on seed requirement posted. Farmer can decide and post fertilizer requirement. He can get the fertilizers from verified companies by Agmarks.

Pesticides

Either due to lack of awareness on infections or a new kind of infection, agmarks will analyse and provide proper pesticides from verified companies to use. Farmer is provided with an interface to upload the infected plant and send it.

Weather

Agmarks provide customized weather information by taking google & IMD Information together. This along with canal water resources information, he can take better decision.

Storage/Warehouse

Agmarks ensures that farmers get updated information on choosing the nearby storage facilities which have space available with informed price well before, so that they can minimize transport costs.

Prices

Agmarks provide three kind of prices with comparison. National Price vs Local Average Price Range Vs Minimum Support Price. With this he can negotiate for Maximum possible price.

Visibility & Awareness

Agmarks aims to give farmers a platform to get visibility for their produce, so that they transact beyond local markets and get the maximum price possible. To bridge the crucial awareness gap in farmers on all aspects to take informed decisions.
